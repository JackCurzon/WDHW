Get Involved

Performers

This is all about your commitment! For our model to work, the performers we partner with must be invested in creating not only social capital, but social change. Our performers take part in every step of the decision process, as we coordinate our model with your concert, and just as important, lead the drive for social change at the event. Through this we can better accomplish the necessary steps to make your event socially responsible, and more effectivly engauge your fans.

I'm in!

Venues

Venues are the backbone to our events. There is a lot that goes into operating in a socially sustainable manner, while also genrating an exciting atmosphere. Hosting a Concert for Connection means taking responsibility for the impact of every element, from energy source and consumption, down to eviormentally friendly concessions. Our practices bring light to the footprint we leave by altering unsustainable practices, while remaining economically reasonable.

I'm in!

Fans

What is a concert without fans? What is a social movement without participation? This is what Concerts for Connections is all about. We want to orchestrate events that allow you to have an amazing experience, while creating social change as a community. If fans rally behind their favoite artists to support their drive for a more sustainable concert, it gives them the power to work with us and thereby for all of us to create social captial in our world.

I'm in!
